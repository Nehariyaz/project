Our Story

Our Heritage

Founded in 1995, Royal Spice carries forward three generations of culinary tradition from the royal kitchens of Rajasthan. What began as a humble family eatery in Mumbai has blossomed into an award-winning dining destination, preserving authentic flavors while innovating for modern palates.

Culinary Philosophy

We believe in "Shuddh Shakahari" (pure vegetarian) as well as expertly crafted non-vegetarian cuisine, all prepared with "Nimrata" (humility). Each dish is made with hand-ground spices, seasonal ingredients, and traditional techniques perfected over decades. Our non-vegetarian dishes follow the highest standards of halal preparation.
